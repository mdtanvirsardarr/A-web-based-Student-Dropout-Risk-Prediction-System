About Dropout Predictor

Dropout Predictor is an AI-driven platform designed to help educational institutions identify students at risk of dropping out. By analyzing academic records, attendance data our models surface actionable insights before it's too late.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to empower teachers and administrators with the tools they need to boost student retention and success.

  • Data-First Approach: We aggregate multiple data sources into a unified dashboard
  • Transparent Models: Our algorithms provide interpretable risk scores
  • Secure & Compliant: Built with student privacy and data protection

Our Mission

To revolutionize student retention through data-driven insights, ensuring every learner has the support they need to succeed academically and beyond.

Vision

A world where predictive analytics helps eliminate preventable student dropouts.

Values

Student-centered, data-informed, and ethically responsible technology.

Impact

Helping institutions improve retention rates by up to 40%.
